Dubai

Dubai is a city in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture, and a lively nightlife scene. Burj Khalifa, an 830m tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline. At its base lies Dubai Fountain, with jets and lights choreographed to music.

The city is also known for its extravagant hotels, such as the Burj Al Arab, which is shaped like a sail and sits on an artificial island. Dubai's shopping scene includes the Dubai Mall, one of the largest malls in the world, featuring an aquarium and an indoor ice rink.
